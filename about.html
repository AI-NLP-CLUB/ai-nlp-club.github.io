AI & NLP CLUB

Artificial Intelligence and Natural language processing (AI & NLP) is a club of NIT Trichy launched in 2018, initiated by the students of CA department under the guidance of Dr. S. Sangeetha in which we welcome everyone to get introduced with the emerging world of Artificial Intelligence along with its branch of Natural Language Processing. This club provides an opportunity for the students to connect with the technologies of artificial intelligence by building up their skills in Natural Language Processing (NLP), machine learning (ML), etc and publicize about creating systems that read, decipher, understand, and make sense of the human languages in a manner that is valuable.
